A NOTE ON THE CONTENT

This is a story about war.
As such, it includes a lot of death (including that of vulnerable populations), and a lot of grappling with one's place in a war-torn world, survivor's guilt, and the ever-looming temptation to give up.

Specific conten warnings include, but may not be limited to: Mild gore, the death of a child, nuclear warfare, food insecurity, allusions to suicidal ideation
